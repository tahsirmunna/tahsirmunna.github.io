About Me

I am a Ph.D. Student in Computer Science at the Faculty of Sciences, University of Porto (FCUP), and a PhD Researcher at LIAAD-INESC TEC in Portugal. My research is situated at the intersection of computer vision and natural language processing, specifically focusing on vision-language grounding, multimodal semantic alignment, and deep learning architectures.

Currently, my research focuses on multimodal alignment and semantic reasoning inside AI systems, with the goal of improving how Large Language Models (LLMs) connect structured text with pixel calculations in vision-language tasks. Previously, I investigated compact visual representations for general-purpose feature extraction at the Institute of Telecommunications (IT), Lisbon, and designed recommender pipelines and knowledge graph embedding workflows.

Core Research Focus Fields

Vision & Language Deep Learning Natural Language Processing (NLP) Medical AI Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning Image Compression & Decoding Knowledge Graphs Multimodal Alignment
